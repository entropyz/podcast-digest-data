# Scott Galloway — 

>  @  | 20VC Interview | 2025-07-13

## Key Takeaways

### 1. How to Win in the New Economy
Establish the perception that you are the market leader Use your reputation to attract cheap capital. Overwhelm the competition with capital.

### 2. Richer and More Successful Women Have Fewer, Not More, Men to Date
Dating opportunities for men increase exponentially the more successful they are, while it stays flat for women. 75% of women say that economic viability is hugely important in a mate, compared with only 25% percent of men. Making more money as a man makes you more attractive; making more money as a woman doesn’t make a difference.

### 3. If a Man Has Not Lived With a Woman by the Age of 30 Their Chance of Substance Abuse Goes Way Up
If a man hasn’t cohabitated or married by the time he is 30, there is a 1 in 3 chance he ends up a substance abuser. Data show that while men are physically stronger, they are emotionally and mentally weaker than women. They need the guardrails of a romantic relationship, strong male involvement, or a good job.

### 4. Why the No Drinking Movement Is Reducing Your Chances of Finding Love
If you ask people to reverse-engineer their closest friendships or learnings around dating, almost everyone said alcohol played a role in a positive way. The anti-alcohol movement & the rising cost of going out are reducing people’s chances of finding love. Swiping on dating apps cannot replace meeting people outside.

### 5. Scott Galloway’s Biggest Lessons on Marriage
Don’t keep score: Decide what kind of husband you want to be & live by it. Express affection and desire. Truly notice and celebrate her life, create environments where strangers applaud her success and achievements.

### 6. Quality Time Is BS, Quantity of Time Is What Matters
Quality time is invented by wealthy people with no time for their kids. You need to have “garbage time” with your kids. These are the magical and intimate moments you have with your kids that only happen if you spend a lot of time with them.

### 7. The Best Way to Get Revenge
I have been f**ked over by business partners before. My mentor, Hamid Moghadam, said, “You want to truly get back at them? Live a f**king amazing life” That is the best revenge.
