# Brendan Foody — Mercor

> Co-Founder & CEO @ Mercor | 20VC Interview | 2025-09-21

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why Mercor Pay Their Talent Three Times What Scale and Surge Pay Their Talent
The average pay for Scale & Surge is $30/hour. Our average marketplace pay rate is $95/hour. Having phenomenal talent & treating them incredibly well is the most important thing in this market.

### 2. Why Model Evals Are Broken
Consumers and enterprises do not care about PhD-level reasoning or Olympiad-level math from models. They want models that could build financial models, research decks, or web apps… They want models that are practically useful in the economy.

### 3. Why Reinforcement Learning Environments Will Subsume the Entire Economy
It does not make sense for humans to be doing monotonous work. They should be building frameworks for the models to do it. That will be a ridiculously exciting transition.

### 4. Why Models Still Need Humans
We still have a long way ahead to superintelligence. Models today have gold medals in Olympiad math but they cannot draft an email. The road to automating the entire economy is paved with humans creating evals for all these workflows.

### 5. Why Being Private Is Better and Advice From Jack Dorsey
Jack Dorsey told me to stay private as long as possible. It allows you to be more long-term oriented. Being public means being more susceptible to being caught up in the quarterly numbers.

### 6. What Developer AI Tools Does Mercor Use and Do They Have Switching Costs
We use Cursor the most, followed by Claude Code. The products are improving so quickly, the distribution will likely change over time. There is surprisingly little switching cost.

### 7. What Is the Most Important Question That Startups Have to Ask Themselves in an Age of AI?
“Will models being dramatically better in one to two years improve your business or worsen it?” This is the single most important question to determine if you’re building a durable & well positioned company for the future.
