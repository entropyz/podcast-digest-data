# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-09-21

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Mission Is More Important Than the Money, but We Will Make a F*ck Ton of Money
Businesses should make money to deliver on a mission. We are a mission-driven company, but we are also a for-profit company. The goal is to make the buyers, sellers, and shareholders happy.

### 2. OPEN Is Fairly Priced and Priced for Potential
Housing is the single largest market in the world. VCs don’t value startups by their current cashflow but by their potential. We will earn the potential.

### 3. OPEN Is a Software Business Not an Asset Heavy Business
We are a software company that happens to have some assets. There’s an asset-light model & an asset-heavy model that both work. The leverage from Opendoor will come from the software.

### 4. Why C Suite Should Only Be Paid in Equity
Their main incentive today is to not get fired. We are creating incentives for executives to be bad at their jobs just to get paid. Equity-only compensation would change that.

### 5. WTF Is Going on With Oracle and OpenAI
This is a levered bet at OpenAI working. Oracle is almost like a proxy OpenAI stock. If OpenAI is successful, they will have $300BN to give to Oracle = stock price goes up.

### 6. Why Adobe Will Be Hit by 10% at Least
Talent: Scott Belsky leaving is a bad sign for AI. Metrics: They reported $5BN of “AI-influenced ARR” #2 is a bad sign of margin:  Any public company quoting billions of AI-influenced ARR does not believe they will have billions of real ARR.
