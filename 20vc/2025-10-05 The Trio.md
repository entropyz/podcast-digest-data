# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-10-05

## Key Takeaways

### 1. $15M Revenue Company With Meh Growth Is 0 Value to a VC
VCs are in the upside option game. A $15M revenue company that’s perfectly good and has reasonable growth is actually of zero value to a VC.

### 2. There Are Two Ways to Price a Deal
You can price a deal based on hope or price a deal based on multiples. When you price on hope, you can lean into crazy valuations that ultimately pays off if growth materializes. When you price on multiples, you anchor to today’s fundamentals: revenue, margins, and comparable companies, just like public markets.

### 3. Board Members Are Wrongly Advising That Triple Double Is Good Enough.
It is terrible advice. Triple, triple, double, double is not good enough in 2025 anymore.

### 4. AI Is It BS or Going to Change the World; One of the Two Will Happen
Option A: Productivity will dramatically change. Companies like OpenAI will easily rake in $200BN-300BN in revenue. Option B: AI is still good, but we will enter a readjustment period. One of them will happen in the next 5-7 years.
