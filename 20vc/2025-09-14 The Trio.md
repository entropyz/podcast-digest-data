# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-09-14

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Elon’s $TRN Pay Package Analysed
The Tesla board believes in 3 things: They want the Elon bet. If they didn’t give him the new pay bump he might leave. They are paying him to double down: $8.5T market cap, $400BN EBITDA, robotaxis… They want Tesla to become the largest market cap company on the planet & Elon is the man to do it.

### 2. Should Brex and Ramp Be Valued Like a Financial Services Company or Like a Technology First Provider?
They have the core dynamics of a financial services company, but the growth rate of a software company. The growth is what’s saving them. Once the growth rate slows, they will be valued like a financial services company.

### 3. The Framework We Use to Analyze Every Single Deal
We ask ourselves 3 questions: Is there a category that can support a big winner? Are these guys going to be one of the winners in the category? Are we getting paid for the risk?

### 4. Why Atlassian Is Threatened and What They Should Do
AI is going to decimate their seat base. AI will do the jobs people using Atlassian are doing today. They need to start making AI-native bets.

### 5. Venture Capital Has Shifted to Late-Stage Growth Investing
20% of venture money today is going into “old-school” venture. 80% goes to late stage/growth investing. The market is changing.
