# Jonathan Ross — Groq

> Founder & CEO @ Groq | 20VC Interview | 2025-10-05

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why the Hyperscalers Have to Keep Spending Recklessly on AI
No one is 100% convinced that in 10 years AI won’t be able to do their job. That is how the hyperscalers feel. The alternative to spending like crazy is to risk being completely locked out of their business.

### 2. The Three Pillars of Improving AI, and Why Compute Is the Easiest.
Algorithms, data, and compute. Algorithms rarely improve. Data is hard to obtain & we haven’t fully figured out synthetic data. Compute is the easiest knob to turn: If you have money & a little patience you are guaranteed to get better results.

### 3. Why Every Large Player Will Have to Build Their Own Chip
What you’re getting isn’t your own chip, it is control over your own destiny. OpenAI, Anthropic, and every hyperscaler will build their own chip.

### 4. Why AI Is Different to SaaS and How Spending More Leads to Better Product
The quality of your SaaS product is fixed by engineering. In AI, your product gets significantly better the more you spend. Token costs are matching a company’s revenue because they can spend more to win users.

### 5. The US Is More Risk Averse Than Europe...
The US is terrified of the risk of omission. When you are a massive growth economy, missing out is more expensive than fumbling something. Europe is incredibly willing to embrace the risk of omission.

### 6. We Need to Bring Back Nuclear and How Japan Is Ahead
Japan has a reputation for being slow at decisions but once they decide they move really fast. They are talking about bringing their nuclear reactors back online. They’ve allocated $65BN for AI and they are going to spend it fast.

### 7. AI Is Going to Cause Massive Labour Shortages
AI will make everything cost less. People will opt out of the economy more: They will work less hours & retire earlier. AI will create entire industries that don’t exist today & we won’t have enough labor.
