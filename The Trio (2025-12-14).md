# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-12-14

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why Anthropic Will Go Out at $500BN for the IPO
Databricks is currently trading at 20x multiple. Assuming Anthropic hit $25BN ARR next year. $25BN x 20 = $500BN Even if they’ve just raised at $350BN, it’s still a good IRR for investors.

### 2. This Is Why Harvey Deal Is Simple; Just Do the Deal
They have 178% NRR and 98% logo retention. Their revenue tripled from $50M to $150M in one year. It’s a no-brainer.

### 3. Will the Models Come After the App Layer, the Simple Test?
If you are investing in an AI app, try and recreate it using the core model. Build a functional version of the app as simply as you can. If you are anywhere close to it, you need to pause.

### 4. Why Are Model Companies Not Fated to Be the Same Business as Airlines
Airlines have high fixed costs, and they are easy to switch. But it is not an awful business. If antitrust allows, we could see model companies consolidate.

### 5. Open Source Chinese Models Are a Real Threat and Are Used by the Best Silicon Valley Startups
Many startups claiming their own LLM actually rely on cheap Chinese open source models. They are a fraction of the cost & good enough. It is a real threat.

### 6. If I Was on the Airwallex Board
I would invest at $8BN under one condition: Figure out a location strategy and relocate out of China within the next 12 months. It is not worth the risk.
