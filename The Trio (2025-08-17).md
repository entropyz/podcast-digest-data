# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-08-17

## Key Takeaways

### 1. How Cursor Gained a Shit Ton of Leverage From GPT-5
A month ago, their gross margins were set by their worst competitor. Now there is another vendor on the block for cheap tokens.

### 2. Why the DOJ Is Stupid in Their Regulation of Google
They only stepped in just when Google was at risk. For the last 15 years Google was grinding everyone down & the DOJ did nothing. Now they are cracking down on their monopoly just when OpenAI is challenging their business model.

### 3. Why It Makes Total Sense for Perplexity to Buy Google Chrome
Chrome itself has no value, but it is a gateway to products that do. Perplexity could become the AI backend & become the default AI engine for Chrome. They would kill to own Chrome’s user base as ChatGPT’s AI engine competitor.

### 4. Three Trends That Mean Palantir Will Crush
They are the AI solution for large corporations. They are the AI solution for defence. They have the administration on their side.

### 5. Mega Layoffs Are Coming and Microsoft and Palantir Are Leading the Way
Alex Karp is aiming for 10x revenue with 10% less people. This is the trajectory all B2B companies are on. Massive scale with less humans. Will this be a new trend for B2B companies? @rabois @afc @jrichlive @nikhil sachdev

### 6. Why Shopify Won and How They Did More With Less
In 2022 they had 11,600 employees. Since then, their revenue is up 91% & they only have 8,100 people That is 91% more revenue with 30% less employees.
