# David Cahn — Sequoia Capital

> Partner @ Sequoia Capital | 20VC Interview | 2025-11-02

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Best Trade of 2025
The best trade of 2025 was the AI power trade. People bet that power was going to be a constraint in AI. We are moving away from dollars to gigawatts.

### 2. Who Are the Winners and Who Are the Losers in AI
Winners? Consumers of compute: overproducing compute lowers prices = margins go up. Losers? Producers of compute: Compute becomes a commodity asset.

### 3. Are the $BN Pay Packages for AI Talent Justified
They are symbolic of the desperation companies need to eke out progress. In tech, increasing the probability of making a trillion dollars by 1% can justify spending billions. We are psychologically biased to overestimate what that percentage of contribution is.

### 4. Does Gross Margin Matter in a World of AI
They are a directional indicator of how much product you’ve built on top of foundational models, but they are not absolutely important. Lots of businesses start with low margins that end up becoming super healthy businesses in the long run. If you have a real, scalable product that delivers a lot of value, your margins will eventually go up as the cost of compute goes down.

### 5. The Rise of the $0-$100M Club and Is Triple, Triple, Double, Double Dead
The best AI companies are scaling from 0 to $100M in revenue extremely quickly. There is so much demand for AI right now, the companies that are smashing PMF are growing faster than before. Triple, triple, double, double is an outdated metric.

### 6. What Makes a Great Defence Company Is to Be a National Champion.
A great defence company fundamentally understands the customer (the govt) & is able to drive a nationwide transformation. There will be a few concentrated winners in each country. We’ll have venture funded R&D companies that get consolidated into the national champions.
