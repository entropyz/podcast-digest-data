# Oren Zeev — Zeev Ventures

> Founding Partner @ Zeev Ventures | 20VC Interview | 2026-02-08

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Best Companies Look Weird and That Way They Have a Head Start
If the company looks weird or wrong, there probably won’t be 15 other startups doing it. You will have 2-3 years without real competition & can build a real moat. If you happen to be right, these will be some of the greatest outcomes.

### 2. Investing Lessons From the Boom of 2021
In 2021, I probably paid 3-4x more than I should have for almost every deal. Some of them are still going to be winners despite overpaying. In any given market, you want to do the best deals you can, and some vintages are going to be better than others.

### 3. 50% of Funds Will Go Out of Business
If you are not a platform, then it is much harder for you to raise. At least 50% of funds today cannot raise or aren’t sure if they’re able to. Many of them are not going to be able to raise.

### 4. My Advice to Founders on Pre-Emptive Rounds
Take the money, but continue to behave as if you didn’t. Companies can be overfunded & lead to a loss of focus. Spend it based on the signals from the market, not the pressure from the boardroom.

### 5. What I Want to See When Leading a Series A:
I want to see real risk reduced, not better-looking milestones. Logos without renewal are just noise. You want to see real product-market fit.

### 6. Why You Shouldn’t Judge a Decision by the Outcome
Like poker, you can have the odds in your favor, make the right decisions, and still lose. Judge the process, not one result, or you’ll learn the wrong lesson. If you make the right decisions, you will win eventually.
