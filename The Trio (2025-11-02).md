# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-11-02

## Key Takeaways

### 1. A16z Is the Red Army of the VC Industry
Red Army’s expression: “Quantity has a quality on its own” When you want to take Berlin, get 2 million people who are ready to die & march forward.

### 2. Analysis on Mercor
One positive & Two negatives: Positive: Market is exploding & Mercor is riding the wave. Negatives: Margins are not great, massive customer concentration. This is a bet that the AI CapEx train will run for 3-4 more years.

### 3. Three Ways to Do Venture Today
You can spray if you’re only doing options. If spraying is the only way you make money, the probability of being wrong is too high.

### 4. The Advantage Multi-Stage Funds Have From a Different Cost of Capital
Multi-stage funds today have enormous amounts of money. They can treat early A & B checks as options. This is a luxury smaller funds don’t have.

### 5. IRR Is Not King and This Metric Is Way More Important
IRR is not the goal, the goal is to maximize multiple while keeping the IRR above a target. Have a target IRR (eg. 25%) then maximize multiple provided the IRR doesn’t dip below the target. Hold winners until IRR nears the floor, then exit and distribute
