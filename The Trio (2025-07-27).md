# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-07-27

## Key Takeaways

### 1. I Would Invest in Anthropic Over OpenAI
There is cap table clarity. They’ve got a working business model. They are exercise pricing power: We are seeing these unprofitable AI models becoming profitable.

### 2. Figma is cheap at this price but it is all part of the strategy.
Their IPO is priced at 14–16× NTM with a phenomenal growth rate. This attracts investors: Low valuation hooks prospects into meetings and fuels demand buildup. Underwriters will “walk up” the price later.

### 3. Anti-Portfolio Trauma is the Psychological Price You Have to Pay for Being in the Game
It is the emotional tax you pay for being in good deal flow. If you’re not seeing 10 great deals you are probably not going to do one great deal. The psychological tax for doing one great deal is you pass on nine deals or you don't win them.

### 4. The Best Investors Meet One Founder per Month That Can Return the Fund
If you are an S-tier investor, you need to meet with one fund-returning founder at least once a month. You cannot do all the deals. You have to meet 8-10 a year to make money.

### 5. Why Mega Funds Are Always Better for the Entrepreneur
They do more deals & have more news flow. They have a good brand: Lower picking ratio x higher deal volume = Constant flow of good news. They have massive amounts of capital: They can win deals, pay more, & have the option to stuff more money in later.
