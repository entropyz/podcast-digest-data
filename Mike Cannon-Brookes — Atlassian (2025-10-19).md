# Mike Cannon-Brookes — Atlassian

> Co-Founder & CEO @ Atlassian | 20VC Interview | 2025-10-19

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Secret to Co-CEOship
Find the sweet spot of shared responsibilities: Scott and I overlap by 60-80%. We interchange roles & functions over various periods of time. You need a clear understanding of roles but also a shared context to make it work.

### 2. We Will Have More Devs and More Software in a World of AI
Software will be cheaper to build & we are not running out of ideas for software anytime soon. There will be more devs & they will be far more efficient. We will create far more technology.

### 3. Why None of the AI Startups Today Have a Moat and Why It Does Not Matter
Everyone is copying each other & the switching costs are near zero. Defensibility in the future will come from value, data, and workflows. Incumbents & startups both have a good shot at winning.

### 4. Does per Seat Pricing Die in a World of AI
There will be 2 logical replacements: per-value delivered & consumption model. Customers hate the consumption model & it is hard to measure per-value outcomes. Seat-based pricing won’t die, but it will likely mix with one of these two.

### 5. The Single Greatest Threat to Atlassian Today?
Our biggest threat is losing our creativity. We want to be a multi-decade technology company. We need to be constantly reinventing ourselves, not defending past wins.

### 6. Fave Quote from Tobi @ Shopify
A founder’s job is to fight the entropy of ambition We cannot be complacent: Fight for every customer and user. Stay hungry, act small: There is a long way to go.
