# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-08-03

## Key Takeaways

### 1. LPs Are Showing GPs They Want You to Ignore Them.
From their actions they are saying they like fund managers who ignore their advice. They want fund managers who say thank you for your advice, I am not going to listen and please can I have more money.

### 2. The Faster You Deploy, the More Relevant You Are. Is That So?
The person who’s doing more deals has more relevance. The more you deploy the more relevance you have.

### 3. Every Developer Is Going to Get $10,000 of Credits
The best developers will be on AI 24/7. Every developer at top tech companies should have $10K of AI credits every month. It is cheaper than hiring any human.

### 4. Why the Monopoly Companies of Old Are Not Monopolies Anymore.
All the big incumbents (Apple, Microsoft, Google…) are monopolies in their own market. They are oligopolies at best in AI. For all the power they brought to the table, OpenAI & Anthropic are making a run in.

### 5. Why the Incumbents Are a Bunch of Rich People on the Back Foot.
Google has executed the best of the four. Apple doesn’t have a working AI product, Microsoft bought OpenAI’s product but doesn’t quite own it. Meta is desperately trying to buy products without a business to justify it. They are not too powerful: They are desperately trying to catch up.

### 6. Should Apple Fire Tim Cook?
The key to Apple winning isn’t acquiring another AI company. They absolutely should already have an AI product already. At some point the board has to ask themselves whether the C-suite has what it takes to win in AI.
