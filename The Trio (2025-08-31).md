# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-08-31

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why Is Salesforce Not Growing Faster if AI Is So Amazing
Salesforce AI + Data Cloud has already reached $1BN revenue. Agentforce is doing as many interactions as our support agents in under a year. It is our fastest growing product & we are going after other data clouds providers.

### 2. What Salesforce Learned From Palantir on Forward Deployed Engineers
Salesforce already has a large sales & systems engineering team talking to customers & building prototypes. We do not have forward deployed engineers who build solutions before signing contracts like Palantir. We are open to adopting that idea.

### 3. What Does It Take for Anthropic to Do a 3x From Here?
A lot has to go right, but a lot is going right. They have one of the fastest growth rates over the last year or two. Even if they decelerate at a normal rate relative to their current growth rate, they are still on track to hit $50BN+ in revenue.

### 4. What Needs to Happen for a16z Plan to Work Out and Why Seed Is the Loss Leader
They need to find a few companies that are outrageous upside performers. They need to stuff $1BN at the right price & they will be fine. Everything else is a loss leader to attract the crowd.

### 5. If You Are Not AI Then This Is the Last Round of Funding You Will Get
I have done several B2B deals over the last 18 months that will do great. My advice to those founders: Don’t expect any money. 80% of people I can refer you to will not take a meeting.
