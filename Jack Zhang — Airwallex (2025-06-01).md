# Jack Zhang — Airwallex

> Co-Founder & CEO @ Airwallex | 20VC Interview | 2025-06-01

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The Best Angel Investment in History
When I was starting Airwallex I met with a girl called Lucy. She decided to invest $1M for 20% of the company. Today that investment is worth over $1BN today.

### 2. Matrix Pulled a Term Sheet and the $BN Mistake
Matrix signed a term sheet for $2M on $10M post and then pulled it. I had already rejected every other VC except Gobi VC, who put the $2M round together.

### 3. Yuri Milner Did Not Ask Me Anything About the Business and Then Gave Me $100M
Yuri asked me a few questions about my personal life, how I grew up etc. And then decided to invest $100M at $1.1BN valuation. We agreed the terms in two hours.

### 4. 100% YoY Growth for 8 Years and Revenue Scaling
We never grew below 100% YoY from 2015 to 2023. We reached $500M ARR last August, $600M in November, then $700M in January. We are still growing 90% YoY.

### 5. The $10BN Investment Meeting With Tencent
I waited for 3 months to meet with Tencent. It was the biggest meeting of my life. Our invoicing product failed during the demo. I thought I was going to lose the deal. Turns out James did not need much convincing: He knew cross-border payments was going to be a large market & convinced Pony to invest the day after.

### 6. I Did Not Have a Budget and Realised We Were Running Out of Money
I did not have financial discipline. I was hiring as fast as possible. At one point I realised we were going to run out of money.
