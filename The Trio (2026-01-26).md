# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2026-01-26

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Clickhouse at $15 Billion: The Deal Analysed
It’s an extreme example of how to take advantage of the AI tailwind. They are a decade-old, open-source tool that became a proprietary cloud product. When AI blew up, everyone was using it already.

### 2. Why Big Companies Cannot Buy Small Companies Easily
When big companies buy small companies, they just get swallowed up. If they do not have enough customers, they will get lost in the big company. They need to buy a company with enough critical mass & heft to go do it’s thing.

### 3. The Best Researchers in AI Can Pick Projects to Work On
The best researchers, the ones you need to win, only want to work on intellectually challenging projects. If you cannot deliver, they will leave. That environment is almost impossible for 99% of software companies to create.

### 4. There Are Three Types of Risk in Venture Capital
Technical & founder risk Business GTM & execution risk Valuation risk is all about growth, persistence, and market size.

### 5. Elon Musk vs. OpenAI: Why It’s a Win-Win for Elon.
Elon is suing OpenAI for up to $134BN. If he wins, he gets the money. If he doesn’t, he has slowed OpenAI down. There are not a lot of downsides aside from legal fees.
