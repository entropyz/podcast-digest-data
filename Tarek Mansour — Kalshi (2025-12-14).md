# Tarek Mansour — Kalshi

> CEO @ Kalshi | 20VC Interview | 2025-12-14

## Key Takeaways

### 1. How Polymarket Have Made Kalshi Better
An industry truly becomes one when there is a rivalry. Without Polymarket we wouldn’t have pushed our product & marketing team as hard as we would have. We are both reaching new heights that we wouldn’t have been able to without each other.

### 2. I Wish We Had Built Brand and Marketing Sooner
My old mindset: Build a great product & people will come. We can do more marketing later. Your product will never be perfect & brand is a muscle you have to learn. You need to do both: Build a great product & a great marketing engine at the same time.

### 3. Which Investor Is Not in Kalshi That Tarek Would Most Like to Have in Kalshi
I have a lot of respect for Micky Malka @ Ribbit Capital. I have a lot of respect for Founders Fund & Altimeter. A lot of the crossover funds are also great.

### 4. What Market Should Exist but Tarek Is Too Scared to List It?
Markets around geopolitical conflicts. These markets provide useful information, but we want to keep our markets orthogonal to harm. You shouldn’t get payouts based on something bad happening.

### 5. How Eric and Kareem at Ramp Have Created the Best Founder Brand
They have done an incredible job at storytelling. They’ve built a brand on their founders, the team, and the quality of their execution. I’ve learned a lot from those two.

### 6. What Makes Alfred Lin So Good?
He hates simplistic statements. His answers tend to be gray & nuanced but are the right answers. He’s a counterbalancing actor: He systematically argues the opposite side to reveal tradeoffs & push toward good decisions. He’s optimistic when things are rough, tough when they’re good, and endlessly consistent.
