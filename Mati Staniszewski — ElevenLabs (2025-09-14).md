# Mati Staniszewski — ElevenLabs

> Co-Founder @ ElevenLabs | 20VC Interview | 2025-09-14

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why OpenAI Will Not Beat ElevenLabs With a Voice Product
There are only 50-100 world-class researchers in voice AI & we have 5-10 of them. Our text-to-speech, speech-to-text & music tools are blowing everyone else out of the water. You need many additional steps to perfect voice AI & OpenAI is not investing as much time there as they could there.

### 2. Why 30-50 People Turned Down the Chance to Make $BNs on This Pre-Seed
They weren’t convinced we could fix the research. They thought voice AI was a small market. They doubted our defensibility against incumbents.

### 3. Why Traditional Media Is So Much Less Valuable Than You Think
We did a post with a major publication & got almost zero conversions. Most of our conversions come from newsletters, Discord, Reddit… You have to spend time where your users are.

### 4. Biggest Advice to Founders on Angel Selection
I ask myself 2 questions: Do they have domain expertise that we don’t have? Can they help validate us in circles we don’t have access to?

### 5. How Nat Friedman Beat Every EU Fund to Win the EU Deal of the Decade
He cold emailed us & I met him in London. He was the only investor who tested our API & gave us feedback. He understood our vision & we were keen to partner with him.

### 6. How a16z Beat Every EU Fund to the Deal of the Decade
They showed us that they cared before they invested. They introduced us to celebrities to work on voice licensing. Bryan flew to London & we signed the preliminary term sheet.

### 7. Why You Should Do Secondary Offerings at Every Round of Financing for Your Team
Almost every round we can, we do a secondary & tender offer for employees with vested stock. We want them to feel like there is liquidity in the company. This is valuable because we are betting on something huge & they should feel like they can take the risk.
