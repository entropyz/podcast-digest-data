# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-07-13

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Biggest Issue in B2B AI
Recruiting talent for B2B AI Companies. All the best people are flocking to the few elite companies. Simply hiring a “VP of AI” for your startup isn’t going to do it.

### 2. Why Mega Stock-Based Comp Is Necessary to Win Today
No one ever said to Winston Churchill, “Did you bring WW2 in on a budget?” Meta’s realized that the AI arms race is existential to them. Once it becomes existential, they will do what they have to do to win.

### 3. Early Momentum Matters More Than Anything in Early AI Markets
For early AI companies, attention begets more attention & begets more attention. If you start ahead & continue to execute, it is extremely hard for others to catch up.

### 4. What Microsoft’s Layoffs Means for Sales Teams
They do not want generalist sales reps who do not know their product to show up to big deals in the age of AI. They prefer having a solution engineer that knows the product & partner with somebody in sales. It wasn’t a “replaced by AI” story; it was a “replaced by better people” story.
