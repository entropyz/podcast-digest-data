# Everett Randle — Benchmark

> GP @ Benchmark | 20VC Interview | 2025-11-16

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why the Mega Funds are Focused on Capital Velocity:
The North Star for General Catalyst & Lightspeed is investment velocity. Talk to the principles and Jr Partners at these firms.... It does not matter what Ravi Mhatre or Hemant Taneja are saying; when you are writing $BN checks, that is your main product. You cannot care as much about Series A because 95% of your profit is going to come from the $Bn you put into Databricks.

### 2. Why We Need a New Taxonomy for AI
AI app companies are meaningfully different than SaaS companies. What you’re seeing with AI app companies is if they’re good with a lot of usage, you have a lot of AI inference in your COGS that you don’t for normal SaaS companies. Instead of talking about gross margin and revenue multiples. I hope we talk about gross profit multiples and absolute gross profit dollars per customer.

### 3. Is it Incongruous to Say: We Want to Be the Best Partner to Founders and Still Fire Founders?
Every story has an immense amount of nuance. Times also completely change, it used to be the norm to push founders out. 2025 is completely different to 2000 and even 2010. But also  if you do end up investing in someone who breaks the law or someone that’s crossed ethical lines, it is your responsibility as a board member to take remediations and action.

### 4. OpenAI at $500BN or Anthropic at $360BN
It is hard to imagine that anything could knock ChatGPT off it’s growth trajectory. The combat will come in coding. OpenAI have done an incredible job with Codex but Anthropic still has an edge on B2B. But I think I would rather do OpenAI at $500BN than Anthropic at $350BN.

### 5. Biggest Investing Lesson from Mary Meeker
She is the most qualitative investor I have ever worked with. She lays out all the sequential numbers historically for a company and then all the numbers going forward. She’s seeing what the company will become on an 8-10 year time horizon. To win, use numbers to drive the narrative and story of an investment.
