# Martin Mignot — Index Ventures

> Partner @ Index Ventures | 20VC Interview | 2025-08-17

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Beware of Gross Margins Being Low at the Start; They Should Be
A lot of businesses have bad margins in the early days. All LLM providers were clear examples of that. If that’s the only thing holding you up, you should ignore it in most cases.

### 2. What Are the Ownership Targets of the Best Fund in the World
Our goal is double digit ownership at exit. When we looked at companies that returned money, we owned close to or more than double digits.

### 3. The Power Law of Venture at Index Ventures
We’ve invested ~$11.5BN across nearly 400 companies in 30 years. We’ve returned close to $30BN & still have $20BN+ in holdings. Most of that is concentrated in 8-9 companies.

### 4. Danny Rimer’s Unwavering Conviction in Figma
He believed Dylan was a truly special founder. He had conviction in Dylan for years when they didn’t have a product. Dylan knew he needed to build enough features to be competitive.
