# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-07-06

## Key Takeaways

### 1. If You Have Not Accelerated Because of AI You Have Failed
“Slow” incumbents like Oracle are signing $30BN deals with OpenAI. Founders had 30 months since ChatGPT launched to do something. If you haven’t grown because of AI yet, it’s not going to happen.

### 2. We Know Our Winners Within a Two Year Period
If they’re at or within 25% of the underwrite plan 2 years in, it hugely increases their chances of success. The probability of a 5x goes from 30% to 70%.

### 3. Why the $2.5BN Melio Outcome Was Discouraging
They had $153M ARR, growing 127% YoY. They only sold for a 16x revenue multiple. It is not a deceleration story: You wouldn’t advise your portfolio companies to sell for $2.5BN with those numbers.

### 4. Is the Money Being Invested in AI Today an Economically Rational Decision
We are investing $300-400BN a year in CapEx alone. This does not include salaries. We do not know if it will be an economically rational decision 2-3 years from now.

### 5. WTF Happens to All These $100M ARR Companies With Low Growth?
Every venture firm has a few of these companies. They’re too small for an IPO, and their slow growth limits their financing options. I am not expecting PE to come in and save the day.
