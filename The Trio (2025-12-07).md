# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-12-07

## Key Takeaways

### 1. How Do You Value Companies When They Re-Accelerate
When companies start re-accelerating at scale, the valuation model breaks. When Anthropic re-accelerated this year, everyone realized the model was wrong. That’s why we saw a step-function increase in valuation.

### 2. Analysis of Eventbrite Acquisition by Bending Spoons
The public-company reality: Slow-growth public companies with low valuations become vulnerable to these acquisitions. Investors with smart money thinks these businesses are still worth buying. It’s very likely PE firms would buy these companies, pair them with a sharp AI startup, and bring back 20% growth.

### 3. The TAM Trap and What It Means for Startups and Public Companies
B2B SaaS founders used to have 4-5 years to figure it out & grow out of their small TAMs. Now, the average public SaaS company is growing at 16% YoY. We’ve never grown more slowly.

### 4. Why SaaS Is Like Japan
It’s a great economy, but the population is shrinking. There are only so many seats to go around.

### 5. What It Takes to Get to $100M ARR Faster Than Ever
Startups are reaching $100M ARR faster than ever & we don’t care how they get there. You cannot hire 1,000 people & brute force your way to $100M ARR in 10 months any more. You need massive inbound demand and AI leverage to hit that pace.

### 6. The Future of Labour Markets Is Fucked
Big companies aren’t hiring because they need to be efficient. Model companies are only hiring a small number of geniuses. Small AI app startups are growing so quickly that they can’t hire fast enough.
