# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-11-08

## Key Takeaways

### 1. The New Normal for What It Takes to Build a BN Business
A $700M+ revenue company, growing 32%, is struggling in its IPO at $5BN The question really is is a $4.5BN dollar business enough in today venture capital

### 2. Why the Navan IPO Proves That Gurley Was Wrong
Navan’s IPO shows allocations aren’t guaranteed “free money.” Issuers can misprice high; stock drops, so buyers demand a protective pop. Most IPOs pop, but rare blowups justify investor discounts and underpricing.

### 3. Why Google Is a Buy and Amazon Is a Sell
Google: Good at consumer AI, search, TPU. Only one making real money other than Nvidia. Amazon: Little to no application layer & hardware, niche search only used for e-commerce. Currently, Google is underappreciated & Amazon is overappreciated.

### 4. Why Now Is the Best Time to Be a Series an Investor and the Worst Time to Be a Seed Investor?
There’s an explosion of seed AI startups. The top-of-funnel is the best it’s ever been. Thousands of founders are coming out of accelerators & they can’t all get funded. Seed is tough because everyone wants to do seed.

### 5. OpenEvidence Seized the Moment and the Moment is Everything
If you built a better version of OpenEvidence a year from now, no one would care. You’ve missed the moment. 90% of the people who will ever adopt the tool are in the market now & they make their decisions quickly.
