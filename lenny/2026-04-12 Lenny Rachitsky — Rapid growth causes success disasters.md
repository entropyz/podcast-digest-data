Kind: captions Language: en Roughly 70% of what I spend my time on is what we internally refer to as success disasters. And that is where things have gone so well that other things are breaking. And I think anyone who's worked at companies that have gone through rapid growth, you think Facebook or Uber or DoorDash early on, they understand this viscerally where scaling this much just brings a lot of challenges each of those categories on acquisition, on activation, on monetization. There's just a ton of firefighting jumping from one urgent thing to another and it's often extremely painful. And it's funny because all the charts are like green and like fully up into the right. It can be quite tough emotionally still. And so you need to sort of step back and just realize like we're very lucky to have these problems.
