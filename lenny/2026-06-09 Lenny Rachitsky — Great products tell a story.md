Kind: captions Language: en Too many times when we're technology-led, we talk about the what. We don't talk about the why. And the why is where the storytelling is. Because you want to take the journey of why it matters to you. Storytelling, it's just so essential to who we are because we like to be taken on a journey. And people love to be educated that way. You can learn all the basics of how to do something. But that doesn't really tie it into something that's meaningful. And so when you tie it to something that's human, that's when it becomes accessible for humans and it's relatable. Hopefully, when you're buying a product, you're taken on a journey. And that's when great marketing, great sales happens. Great storytelling through the product design, that's even better. When it sings from the depths of the product that you could feel the love and care, that's when people go, "Oh, I love this and I want more from that brand or from that company or that team."
