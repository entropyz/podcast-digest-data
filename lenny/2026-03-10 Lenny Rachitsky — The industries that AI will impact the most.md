Kind: captions Language: en I think the real impact of AI in the next 5 to 10 years really is going to be in farming, in mining, in construction. These industries, they need autonomy and it couldn't come soon enough. If you look at farmers, average age of a farmer is in their late 50s. What does that mean in 10 years from now? Many of those farmers are going to be retiring if they're not already retired. In the 1980s and the 1990s, doing the long haul trucking job was what the family has to sacrifice, the father not being there. That is where I think this intelligence kind of revolution in the real world is really I think going to fill those gaps in rather than an entire industry suddenly gone.
