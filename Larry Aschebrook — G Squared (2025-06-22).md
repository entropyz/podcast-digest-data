# Larry Aschebrook — G Squared

> Founder & MP @ G Squared | 20VC Interview | 2025-06-22

## Key Takeaways

### 1. This Firm Made BNs on Spotify From a 22 Year Old Analyst
One of my first employees suggested buying some shares in Spotify. We flew to Stockholm to get approved as shareholders & they asked if we could buy $150M worth of shares. We didn’t have the money & only closed $125M on the fund.

### 2. Spotify Was 40% of the Fund...
We were buying up as many shares as possible. We became a top 10 shareholder of Spotify: $380M. It became a $1BN outcome for our LPs, but it was a very concentrated risk.

### 3. How We Made $800M on Coursera
We were the largest shareholders of Coursera through our strategy. We owned 16% of Coursera when it went public. We sold at $36/share and returned $800M to our LPs.

### 4. What I Learned From Being Sued by Theranos
We signed up for their secondary but something didn’t feel right. We tore up the binding form. They threatened to sue & I personally settled for a few million dollars. It was a bad process & bad outcome.

### 5. The Biggest Advice to Managers Raising Funds Today
Create a firm that is different. The world does not need another fund. You have to stand out.

### 6. Don’t Chase the MOIC, Chase F**king Cash Distributions
We made a 2x net over a five year period. Cash returns, not shares. We stopped distributing shares because LPs will blame you if they hold it.

### 7. The Whole Problem of Silicon Valley
People have lost focus on what they were hired to do. They pay more attention to the lifestyle that comes with being a money manager. At the end of the day it is all about the value you are creating for the people who have entrusted you with their money and making a difference for your underlying companies.
