# Vlad Tenev — Robinhood

> Co-Founder & CEO @ Robinhood | 20VC Interview | 2025-07-20

## Key Takeaways

### 1. WTF Are You Buying When You Buy OpenAI Tokens on Robinhood
It is like a stable coin: assets sit in custody, get tokenized 1:1, and trade on-chain. We have access to private company shares or convertible interests in a private entity (like OpenAI) These tokens get put on blockchains where they’re tradeable.

### 2. Was OAI Wrong to React Badly to RH Tokenising Their Stock
The statement that it was unsafe was not very positive for us. Everyone likes tokenization in principle. But it is not as attractive when it is being done to you.

### 3. How to Solve the Access Problem With the Extension of Private Markets
The best private companies can raise money from anywhere & not consider retail. This leads to an adverse selection problem: Companies going into retail don’t have any other options. It is important for tokenization to work without the opt-in of companies that are being tokenized.

### 4. What Will Be the Biggest Revenue Line for Robinhood in the Next 5 Years
Most people view crypto as a separate layer of our business today. It will likely be a new layer that powers everything. Interest on stable coins, prediction markets, etc…

### 5. The Majority of Robinhood’s Code Is AI Generated
Nearly all of the code is written by AI. The adoption rate within our engineering team is close to 100%. We have little to no human generated code.

### 6. Should Stripe Go Public?
They can do whatever they want; they can raise infinite capital from the private markets. It is a big problem that retail investors can’t get access to Stripe shares. I’ve shared my viewpoint with Patrick & John & they asked me not to tokenize their shares.

### 7. The Only Public Company CEO Who Actually Likes Being Public
I think of being public as a fun opportunity to connect with our community of shareholders. When we do an earnings call we treat it like a post-game conference. They are fun whether or not the team wins or loses.

### 8. Which Competitors Does Vlad Most Respect and Admire
Revolut: No BS, cares a lot about winning. Coinbase: Founder-led, doing very well. Behind the scenes:
