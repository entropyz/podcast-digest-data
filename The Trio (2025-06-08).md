# The Trio

> Market Commentary | 2025-06-08

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Multi Stage Makes Life Harder for Everyone
“The existence of people with $10BN to spend makes it hard for people with $600M to spend it rationally.”

### 2. Two Core Elements to Do Seed Well
#1: You cannot sell the things that matter. #2: Be honest with yourself if the thesis breaks. You have to learn how to sell. Learning how to sell is just as important as learning how to buy.

### 3. It Is BS That if You Are Not in the Two to Three That Matter You Are Lighting Money on Fire
Insight made $400M for their investors on Hinge Health 20% of $400M is $80M. People will still cash the check even if it is 3x: $80M is still real money.

### 4. AI Has Turned Cash Cows Into Cash Pits
The hyperscalers have turned cash-efficient businesses into CapEx hogs. Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, Google have all increased their CapEx spend. The investors don’t mind right now because their non-AI business cashflows have gone up as well.

### 5. Public Company Investors are Mean VCs on Steroids
One moment they’ll call the team in & say double the burn. Six months later they’ll ask why are you spending so much money? If the growth rate slows down on the hyperscalers we will likely see pressure to tone down the CapEx spend.
