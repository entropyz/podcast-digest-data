## Key Takeaways

### 1. Why Windsurf Could Be a Much Better Company in 90 Days Post-acquisition.
Windsurf was hopeless before this deal: They lost 40 S-tier developers. It would be impossible to hire 40 S-tier developers in such a short timespan. Cognition has the talent: Their acquisition was a lifeline.

### 2. Why the Lovable Market Is So Much Bigger Than Anyone Appreciates
There is a large number of developers who finish their code on Lovable or Cursor. There is a huge overlap between developers and consumers who use Lovable. If Lovable can make both groups happy, they could be bigger than Cursor & Windsurf.

### 3. Why These AI Deals Are Actually Cheaper Than You Think.
We have seen undifferentiated companies raising at $300M pre-money with less than $1M in revenue. We have seen investors doing 200-300x ARR deals. Windsurf is selling for 20x revenue, and Lovable is raising at 20x.

### 4. Founders Are Fucking Cashing in Faster and More Than Ever
They were raising at $3BN but did not hesitate to grab the $2.6BN acquisition. This is happening There are a lot of hype companies where the person who knows the most is going for the cold hard cash.

### 5. Why Grok Could Win the Foundation Model Game
Elon has close to unlimited capital. He knows AI from cofounding OpenAI & Tesla. He knows how to acquire talent & assemble an S-tier team.

### 6. Why Every Public Company Will Buy Bitcoin in the Next 24 Months.
It’s mainstream, it’s liquid & has some benefits in the market. CFOs will start holding bitcoins as part of their treasury. Everyone will have some level of exposure.
